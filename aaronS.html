In the depths of Discord, where Aarons abound,
Each seeking free games, their wallets compound
"Yo, where's that girl dev?" they eagerly chase,
Their NFT avatars flooding cyberspace

Their tokenized hopes gradually sunk
"Wen airdrop?" they cry in channels galore,
While their crypto bags keep dropping more

The girl dev just laughed as she built something real,
While Aarons kept searching for that perfect deal
Their Web3 dreams of riches untold,
Turned out to be copium, if truth be told

So here's to the Aarons, still searching today,
For free alpha, airdrops, and games they won't play
Their wallets are empty, but hope springs eternal,
In Discord they dwell, in FOMO infernal
